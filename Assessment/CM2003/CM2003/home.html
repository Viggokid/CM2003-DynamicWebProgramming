About

Walking is great exercise but sometimes we don’t know where to go and if you walk the same routes a lot then they can became boring and uninspiring. But with these routes you can walk in Aberdeen you can see the scenery and also get great exercise. So get your boots on rain or shine (most likely rain) and take your family and friends. Have a great day out whether you are local looking for a cheap and active day out or a tourist wanting to break up the usual routine of museums and heritage sites ( I am sure that the kids will thank you for that one). Take a look at our routes and choose one (or both!) for a great active day out. Plus with our great routes you won’t need to go to a sweaty gym and waste your hard earned money and you will see something new and find some exciting new areas in Aberdeen that you have never heard of. You could make a treasure map with the routes as a good way of getting kids active and also having fun. We hope you have fun and that the sun shines for you.
